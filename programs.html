Programs

Education

At NTF we provide books and supplies to help struggling teens stay in school and reach their highest potential.

Football

We provide young people with the opportunity to play in our Teens League; players from 13 to 16 years of age can register. .

Music

We provide a program where youths are given an opportunity to learn aspects of the entertainment industry.

Technology

At New Teens Foundation, teens and young adults are developed in the area of technology through comprehensive training

Modelling

The New Teens Foundation develops teens and young adults in the area of modelling.We help them become more creative and independent.

Drama/Movies

Our Unique program helps young adults and teenagers explore the world of drama and movie-making.

About Us
